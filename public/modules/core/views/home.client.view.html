Only 3 steps to use.

1) You have to Sign Up.
2) Now go to Research -> New Research, complete the requiered fields as you want and submit.
3) Relax, you're done! Now you just have to wait: you will receive an email confirming that your new research has been started.

Build your personal dashboard.

You are free to build your own dashboard of datavisualization.

You can choose between a lot of charts, boards and timelines to get back all the information you want.

Text analysis provided.

All the contents Pandata tracked are analysed in order to give you a precise sentiment analysis.

You will know what people truly think over the subject you chose.
